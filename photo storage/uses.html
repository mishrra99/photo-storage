How to Use the Photo Upload Gallery

Welcome to the Photo Upload Gallery!

This platform allows you to upload your favorite photos and manage them easily. Follow the steps below to get started:

Step 1: Fill in Your Details

In the upload section, you will find fields to enter your details:

  • Secret Key: Enter a secret key (e.g., xyz, 0-9, any words) to identify your uploads.
  • Instagram ID: Enter your Instagram ID for easy access to your profile.
  • Sortlist Name: Provide a name for the sortlist under which your photo will be categorized.

Step 2: Upload Your Photo

Click on the "Choose File" button to select a photo from your device. The gallery accepts image files in the following formats:

  • .jpg
  • .png
  • .gif

After selecting the photo, click the "Upload Photo" button to add it to your gallery.

Step 3: View Your Photos

Your uploaded photos will be displayed in the gallery section. Each sortlist will show a cover photo and allow you to expand to see all photos under that sortlist.

Step 4: Interact with Your Photos

You can interact with your photos in the following ways:

  • Click on a photo: This will open your Instagram profile in a new tab.
  • Hover over a photo: A tooltip will appear with a message.
  • Delete a photo: Click the "Delete" button to remove a photo. You will need to confirm your identity by entering your secret key.

Step 5: Refresh Your Gallery

Whenever you upload or delete photos, the gallery will automatically refresh to display the latest changes.
